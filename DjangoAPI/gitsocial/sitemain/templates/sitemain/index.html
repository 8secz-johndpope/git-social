About

Git Social brings social gamification to open source. Share your contributions, earn badges, and raise on the leaderboards.

You can create an account, linking your GitHub, and then use both the Web App and Mobile App to view your contributions. You can also search for other user's contributions and be linked to them through social media posts. Our app allows sharing your contributions and earned badges to SnapChat as stickers that can be attached to images.
